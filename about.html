Who I Am

I am a fresh web designer and developer living in Bekasi, Indonesia.
I came from a physics background where I focused on material science. My desire to design and coding stems from my affinity for geometry and algorithm. Over the last few years, I have encouraged myself to delve into design and coding. With the same principle on science, I want to promote innovation as a designer and developer.

Passion

Science

Science is one of the most important channels of knowledge. Well-developed science is just like magic, it can open up a lot of possibilities. I love science since I was little, and I want to promote innovation as a designer and developer in the same way a researcher is with science.

Design

There is a phrase "One should be in harmony with his or her environment, and things should be made to last rather than be replaced". If a product or service is a solution to a problem, then it is the role of design to make it in harmony with the user and make it last.

Coding

Programming is not just about writing code, it is a systematic way of reasoning. Every step is a formula to construct marvelous magic. It is wondrous to see the beauty of the design made by detailed calculations and codes.
